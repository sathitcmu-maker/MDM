Job Description
Define the overall direction and policies of the Management Division to align with the company's strategic goals.
Oversee corporate image management and maintain relationships with external organizations and government agencies.
Supervise all corporate legal matters, including reviewing business contracts, MOUs, and legal binding documents, as well as managing contract renewals and document retention.
Oversee and control all business-related licenses, permits, and certifications to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.
Manage HR strategies and compensation structures aligned with business direction, and provide labor law consultation.
Study and propose organizational restructuring and capability development to support sustainable growth and IPO readiness.
Supervise all administrative functions and company-wide asset management.
Define digital strategies to support business and manufacturing operations while overseeing cybersecurity and IT infrastructure.
Explore the application of innovation, digital technologies, and Gen AI to drive efficiency and competitiveness.
Monitor regulatory updates and assess business impact to ensure compliance.
Analyze and assess legal risks and prepare reports with recommendations.
Ensure proper governance of Board of Directors' registers, shareholder registers, and other critical corporate documents.
Prepare documents, reports, and presentations in Thai and English, and coordinate with internal/external parties.
Perform duties assigned by the CEO or the Board of Directors.
Manage the division's budget aligned with company policies.
Execute other assigned tasks.
Qualification
Master's degree or higher in Business Administration, Law, Human Resources & Organization Management, or related fields.
Experience working with leading organizations in Thailand or multinational companies.
Minimum 15 years of experience in business law and general management, with at least 10 years in an executive role.
Strong communication skills with excellent command of English.
High attention to detail and ability to work under pressure.
Strong multi-tasking and time management skills.
Experience in the food industry or a publicly listed company is an advantage.
Role Clarity: Every duty in this JD maps to one of three executive layers — Set Direction (policies, strategy, digital direction), Govern & Coach (supervise legal, control licenses, oversee IT/HR), or Accountability (report to CEO/BOD, manage budget, prepare Board documents). This is not a doing role — it is a directing and governing role across three departments.
